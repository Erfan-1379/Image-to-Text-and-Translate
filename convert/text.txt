Python is an easy to learn, powerful programming language. It has efficient high-level data structures and a
simple but effective approach to object-oriented programming. Python's elegant syntax and dynamic typing,
together with its interpreted nature, make it an ideal language for scripting and rapid application development in
many areas on most platforms.

Django is a high-level Python web framework that encourages rapid development and clean, pragmatic
design. Built by experienced developers, it takes care of much of the hassle of web development, so you can
focus on writing your app without needing to reinvent the wheel. It’s free and open source.
